    Finding a stay that’s right for you

    Whether you seek reassurance before booking or need to cancel promptly, locating the cancellation policy for your stay is essential. Here's how to access it:

    Before you book

    Cancellation information is readily available on the listing page and throughout the booking procedure, accessible to you before making any payments.

    After your book

    Your policy and cancellation choices are conveniently accessible in your Trips section. By clicking or tapping "Show trip details," you can easily locate your Cancellation policy. Please note, the displayed times and dates for cancellation policies adhere to the local time zone of the listing. Refund deadlines are calculated from the check-in time specified for the listing in its local time zone, or 12:00 AM if no check-in time is specified. For insight into your refund breakdown, initiate the cancellation process, and we'll provide you with a comprehensive breakdown. Depending on your reservation's duration, timing of cancellation, and the applicable cancellation policy, canceling after check-in might result in a partial refund. Explore more about various cancellation policies to gain a deeper understanding. It's important to keep in mind that the refunded amount will never exceed the actual amount you've paid at the time of cancellation. Delve further into understanding refund amounts for more detailed information.

    Extenuating Circumstances

    If an emergency or natural disaster has affected your reservation, you might qualify for a refund under extenuating circumstances. If your cancellation pertains to the COVID-19 pandemic, familiarize yourself with the available options and understand what applies to your situation.

    Cancellation policies for Hosts

    If you are a Host or want to learn more about what cancellation policies are available, please refer to cancellation policy for your listing.

    Issues while you’re on a trip

    Should you face challenges upon arrival at your listing that the host cannot promptly resolve, you may be covered by our Rebooking and Refund Policy for suitable assistance.
