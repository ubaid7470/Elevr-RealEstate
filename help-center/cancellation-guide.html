    Finding a stay that’s right for you

    Occasionally, unforeseen circumstances arise, necessitating a cancellation. To maintain operations seamlessly, here's the procedure for canceling a reservation, determining your refund, or understanding the steps to take if situations don't unfold as expected.

    If you need to cancel

    Let's swiftly handle your cancellation: If it's due to a change in your plans, navigate to Trips to cancel the reservation. As you proceed with the cancellation process, the refund amount will be displayed. In the case of a stricter cancellation policy at your booked place, an alternative could be adjusting your dates. Simply submit a change request to the Host, and any price difference will be presented before confirming the request. Additionally, consider messaging the Host to provide more details about your circumstances and reasons for modifying the reservation.

    If your host cancels

    Occasionally, a Host might need to cancel your reservation, though it's uncommon. If such an instance occurs, rest assured—we'll expedite your refund and assist you in locating a comparable or superior home for rebooking.

    Receiving your refund

    While we aim to expedite the process, the timing and amount of your refund are contingent upon your reservation's cancellation policy and the timing of your cancellation, except in cases of extenuating circumstances. It's crucial to review your reservation for specific details. The positive aspect is that if you qualify for a refund, it will be automatically processed within 10 days to the payment method initially utilized for the reservation
