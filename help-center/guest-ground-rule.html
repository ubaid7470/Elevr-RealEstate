    Ground Rules for Guests on Evelr

    Violation Reporting

    • Cleanliness: Avoid excessive mess requiring deep cleaning. Cleaning fees cover standard cleaning between reservations.
    • Litter: Dispose of trash in designated areas without excessive amounts.
    • Damage: Inform Hosts promptly of any damage, working towards a reasonable solution.

    Follow Host’s House Rules

    • Approved Guests: Respect guest limits and avoid disruptive gatherings.
    • Check-In/Out: Adhere to check-in/out times specified by the Host without exceptions unless approved.
    • Smoking: Abide by smoking policies set by the Host within the listing.

    Additional Guidelines

    • Pets: Adhere to pet policies, not bringing more or different pets without approval.
    • Noise: Respect quiet hours and avoid disturbing neighbors with excessive noise.
    • Commercial Use: Avoid film or photography for commercial purposes without Host permission.

    Violation Reporting

    • Hosts should promptly report violations to Evelr, documenting issues for resolution.
    • Contact Evelr for support in reporting or seeking reimbursement for damages through the Resolution Center.
    • Honest reviews with feedback help improve future stays.

    Enforcement and Consequences

    • Evelr enforces these rules, issuing warnings or suspensions for repeated or severe violations.
    • Hosts may have additional rules on their listing page; violations lead to appropriate action with support from Evelr.
    • Guests can appeal decisions by providing additional information for review.

    Effective Communication

    • Responsive communication between Hosts and guests is crucial for successful stays. Evelr encourages prompt responses to resolve issues.

    Guests have the responsibility to respect the Host's property and comply with their rules, ensuring a positive experience for all parties involved.
