    Getting started on Evelr

    Whether you want to book a stay away from home or host an Evelr experience in your own place, here’s how Evelr works for you. Let’s start with the basic and go from there. Welcome to the Evelr community!

    The Evelr Community

    Evelr is a community built on fostering connections and a feeling of belonging. We place a high priority on the safety of our Hosts and guests—Hosts are required to meet and maintain quality benchmarks, and all personal profiles and listings undergo thorough verification. Our commitment to ensuring everyone's safety on our platform includes encrypted personal data and a reliable payment system. Additionally, our 24/7 community support team is ready to assist with any questions you may have.

    Getting Set Up

    Signing up is simple and free! We'll just require some basic information and a government ID, which we keep confidential and don't share with Hosts or any other parties. Once completed, you're all set to make your bookings!

    Hosting basics

    If you're considering becoming a host for your place, we'll provide you with a comprehensive, step-by-step guide through the process. For hosting Experiences, simply familiarize yourself with our quality criteria, craft a listing, and present your captivating idea for review, either through a form or a brief video if it's an online experience.
