    Paying for your trip

    When does the payment for a reservation occur? What steps should you take if you're unable to finalize your transaction? Let's delve into the financial aspects and find solutions to these crucial inquiries.

    Basics of Payments

    You're prepared to confirm your booking but require payment. The great news is we accommodate various payment methods, contingent upon the country where your payment account is situated. If you need to divide your payment, it's possible provided your stay qualifies and a payment plan option is available during checkout. It's essential to note that regardless of the payment method or timing, ensure all transactions are conducted through Evelr.

    Paying for Reservations

    Congratulations on successfully making a reservation! Wondering what comes next? Your payment method will be automatically charged once your reservation is confirmed, excluding any security deposits. Facing difficulties with payment? Ensure your credit card isn't expired, or consider reaching out to your bank for assistance.
