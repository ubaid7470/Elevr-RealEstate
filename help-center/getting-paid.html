    Paying for your trip

    Congratulations! You've rightfully earned it. So, when can you expect to receive it? Here's essential information regarding payments and ensuring you receive your earnings according to your preferences.

    Payout Set-Up

    Upon becoming a Host, it's essential to establish a payout method for your account. The setup process may take some time to complete, so we advise doing this promptly. But how do you select your payout currency? It's determined by the country and method you initially chose while adding your payout method. Note that once a payout method's currency is set, it cannot be altered. However, you have the flexibility to create a new payout method in a different currency whenever necessary.

    Amount and timing

    Normally, Evelr releases your payout approximately 24 hours following your guest's planned check-in time. The duration for the funds to reach your account varies based on your chosen payout method and factors such as bank holidays or weekends. Moreover, if you've set a minimum payout threshold or are hosting an extended stay, it might extend the payout duration or result in a different payout schedule.
