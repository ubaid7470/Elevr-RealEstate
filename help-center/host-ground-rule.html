    Ground Rules for Hosts on Evelr

    • Reservation Commitment: Hosts are expected to honor accepted reservations and ensure a reliable check-in experience.
    • Timely Communication: Hosts or Co-Hosts should respond promptly to guest inquiries and issues before and during stays.
    • Listing Accuracy: Accurately describe the property and its features during booking, including details, location, type, size, privacy, amenities, and house rules.
    • Listing Cleanliness: Ensure the property is clean, safe, and free of health hazards before guest check-in.

    Maintaining high review ratings is essential. Hosts should focus on commitment, communication, accurate listings, and cleanliness.

    Violation Reporting

    • Guests are encouraged to report violations promptly and communicate with the Host to resolve issues.
    • Document any issues using Evelr's message thread or photos.
    • If unresolved, contact Evelr or request a refund through the Resolution Center and leave an honest review.

    Enforcement & Consequences

    • Evelr will investigate reported violations and may warn, suspend, or remove Hosts or listings for severe or repeated violations
    • Actions may include canceling reservations, refunding guests, or requiring Hosts to address issues before resuming hosting.
    • Hosts can appeal violations by contacting customer support or using the provided link, providing relevant information for review.
